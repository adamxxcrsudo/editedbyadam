— Process

How a project gets cut

Every job runs down the same timeline — the playhead below tracks it as you scroll.

  1. 00:00:00:00

    Import Footage

    Raw media comes in, gets backed up and organised before a single cut is made.

  2. 00:04:12:00

    Organise

    Bins, labels and proxies — footage is sorted by scene, take and usability.

  3. 00:11:30:00

    Rough Cut

    The first pass — structure and story over polish, getting the shape right.

  4. 00:22:05:00

    Fine Cut

    Trimming for pace, tightening transitions, locking the edit's rhythm.

  5. 00:31:48:00

    Motion Graphics

    Titles, lower thirds and kinetic type built to match the cut's energy.

  6. 00:40:20:00

    Colour Grade

    A consistent look applied across every shot for mood and continuity.

  7. 00:48:55:00

    Audio Mix

    Dialogue cleaned, music balanced, sound design layered for impact.

  8. 00:55:00:00

    Final Delivery

    Exported, QC'd and delivered in every format the platform requires.
